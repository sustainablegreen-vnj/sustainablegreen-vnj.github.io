3R = Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

Reduce, Reuse, and Recycle - we've all heard of this before, but what does it really mean?

  • Reduce the amount of waste you create
  • Reuse as much items as you can before you replace it
  • Recycle used items to give them a new purpose
