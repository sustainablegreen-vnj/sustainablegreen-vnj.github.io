3R = Reduce, Reuse, Recycle









     Reduce, Reuse, and Recycle (3R) are concepts on how to correctly manage waste. Those concepts are:

    1. Buying products that you really need and better quality so they can last longer.

    2. Replace the use of plastic bags, utensils, water bottles for more environmentally friendly paper bags, recyclable utensils, and your own water bottle.

    3. Choose products that are environmentally safe to discard, or have less environmentally damaging packaging.

    4. Buying items you can reuse and to reuse them as much as possible.

    1. Jars, plastic or glass can really be helpful, especially for use in the kitchen.

    2. Plastic bags are extremely durable, and if you’re also trying to reduce the trash you output, reusing plastic bags can be really beneficial.

    3. Give away your old clothes so they can have a new use.

    An added benefit on reuse is the fact that you can reduce your spending by reducing the amount of things you buy.

    1. Making a pot out of old plastic bottles.

    2. A book holder from old items.

    3. And another thing we'd like to mention Is art. There’s just something beautifully ironic about the way something so useless can be turned into a symbol of redemption.





These three concepts are crucial to save earth. People don't realize that implementing 3R isn't that complicated, and we'd want to bring it to light so that people can implement 3R into their lives. They also don't realize that you don't need to do anything grand or magnificent, but just implementing these concepts, even just a little bit into your life can mean everything.


But how can you help directly?



An example is by helping The Greeneration Foundation (GF), which is an environmental foundation that focuses on issues of sustainable consumption and production in Indonesia. The Green Fund Digital Philanthropy (GFDP) is a digital environmental restoration initiative which is owned by GF.



What does GFDP acctually do and why should you donate to them?



  1. The Citarum River is considered to be one of the most polluted rivers in the world. With wooden boats, 70 waste pickers can remove 5.600 kilograms of trash from the Citarum River. By donating, you help support the valiant efforts of these waste pickers. You help the purchase and creation of boats for them and help cover the operational costs needed to clean the Citarum River.

  2. Many of the waste pickers in Indonesia are informal workers. They work al day, just to get paid with minimal wage. For this reason many of the families can only afford to cover their basic needs, and not enough to pay for their children's school tuition. By donating, you not only help support the waste pickers, and you also help them provide for their children's school needs.
