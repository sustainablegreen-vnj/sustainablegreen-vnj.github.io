3R = Reduce, Reuse, Recycle


     We've all heard of this before, but what does it really mean? And how do we implement it in our lives?

     The first R, Reduce is all about creating less waste. A few examples can be to only buy what you need, to choose products with less packaging, buying items in bulk, look for items you can reuse, and replace usage of single use plastic bags with a cloth bag.

    The second R, Reuse is how you can use certain items multiple times before throwing it away. A few examples can be to repurpose jars and containers, and to donate old clothing and furniture instead of throwing them away.
