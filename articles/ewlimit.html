Energy and Water Limit

We as a society consume a high amount of energy in the form of electricity and fuel (1). We also consume a lot of water globally (2). In a nutshell, we consume a lot of important and vital resources. Without energy, our cities would collapse and run like cavemen. Without water, we all would die. Not all the world has access to these resources though. Africa is one such place where clean and drinkable water is unobtanium (3, 4). Clean water is very scarce in Africa. We should be thankful that we have readily available clean and drinkable water, but some people waste water as if water is an everyday thing everywhere in the world. Wasting water is not appreciating the fact that we have clean and drinkable water. We should be grateful for it. We can appreciate our access to water and energy by not wasting it.
